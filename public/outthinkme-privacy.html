Privacy Policy for OutThinkMe

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

OutThinkMe ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

When you use OutThinkMe, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Game interactions, scores, achievements, and app feature usage
  • Analytics Data: App performance, crash reports, and general usage patterns

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal names or email addresses (the app uses anonymous authentication)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Photos or media

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Save your game progress and achievements
  • Improve and optimize the app experience
  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services

Third-Party Services

OutThinkMe uses the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Anonymous sign-in to save your progress
  • Cloud Firestore: Store game data and progress
  • Analytics: Understand app usage and improve features

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and use advertising identifiers to show relevant ads.

Google Advertising Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Data Retention

Your game data is retained as long as you use the app. If you uninstall the app and do not reinstall within 180 days, your anonymous account and associated data may be automatically deleted.

Children's Privacy

OutThinkMe is rated for ages 4+ but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app uses anonymous authentication and does not require any personal information to use.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete your game data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Request information about your data by contacting us

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections and stored in secure cloud infrastructure provided by Google Firebase.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
